## [DocMaster: A Hierarchical Structure-Aware System for Document Analysis](https://doc-master.github.io/)

DocMaster parses documents into hierarchical document trees preserving original layouts and constructs a structure-aware semantic index that enables accurate document filtering and in-depth analysis. See the paper website [here](https://doc-master.github.io/).

## [MAS-Designer: Optimizing Multi-Agent Communication Topologies via GRPO]

Formulated multi-agent communication topology optimization as a graph pruning problem and solved it via Group Relative Policy Optimization with multi-sample averaged rewards.
